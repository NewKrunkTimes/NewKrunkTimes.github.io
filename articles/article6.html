New Krunk Times

New Killstreaks and Secondary!

Article By iamanenvelope

Tuesday, 17 May 2022

Even more weapons are going to be added to the game very soon! Many people have been asking for more content in krunker and boy are they delivering. Tehchy has been revealing more new kill streaks and weapons over the past few days. Some lower tier secondaries and killstreaks have already been added to the game, but now it’s time for the real game changers.

Refill

This one is going to be a slot 1 killstreak and is probably the most requested killstreak or update to knives since knife throwing came out. Yes, you will finally be able to refill your knife for another throw. With this you can refill your knife when you reach a five player killstreak. You most likely will only get one refill as it would be a little overpowered to let players throw their knives once every five kills, but it is a possibility.

War Machine

The War Machine will probably be a slot 2 killstreak. It will probably be called in on a 15 killstreak. The War Machine is a grenade launcher with similar physics to the Noob Toob secondary. It has 6 ammo and will disappear once you launch all 6 shots. A direct hit from the grenade does at least 100 damage so it can one shot most classes. The bomb explodes on impact and the explosion is larger than a Noob Tube or RPG explosion. It does anywhere from 40-60 damage to players depending on where they are in the explosion so you can kill most players in 2 shots if you do not hit them directly. This will most likely do damage to the person shooting these explosions as well if you are hit by your own explosion so be careful!

Bombardment

First revealed on a stream by Zomballr, the Bombardment kill streak has been in the works for weeks now. Bombardment will be a slot 3 kill streak and classic nuke but it gets the job done. Instead of instantly killing everything alive, an airstrike flies overhead and drops a rain of bombs on the entire map. The bombs explode on the ground dealing damage to everyone who couldn’t find proper cover. The damage is similar to the War Machine, it can kill people from explosion damage in 2 shots and a direct hit can most likely one shot.

Akimbo Secondary

A new secondary is going to be added as well, we do not know the name of this but it appears to be quite powerful. This secondary looks like the uzis as you hold 2 of them, one in each hand. You most likely can not use ADS with this secondary similar to uzis and has a similar range as well. It is not automatic like the pistol but its fire rate is slower. Headshots deal 16 damage at the closest range and body shots do 9. The damage depends a lot on range though and this secondary is only really effective up close.

Remember that everything is still in testing and all are subject to change.
