Krunker Just Released 2 New Killstreaks!

Article By iamanenvelope

Friday, 13 May 2022

The latest Krunker update have brought 2 brand new killstreak powerups and/or weapons. Krunker has had many updates to killstreaks recently, including the new customization system for selecting killstreaks. 2 new ones were added last week.

Slimer

The first of these is a Slot 2 killstreak called the Slimer. The Slimer can replace the airdrop and can be called in on a 10 player killstreak. The killstreak is very different from the classic nuke or airdrop, as it is a whole new weapon. This weapon shoots slime stains that boosts players off of them (similar to a boost-pad). The weapon lets the player shoot twice, and once both of these shots are used, the weapon disappears. Many players think this killstreak is useless but s some of the more experienced movement players are excited for this new killstreak as it can make for some exciting new (out)plays and awesome trickshots. It may be worth mentioning that the slime shots disappear after a couple of seconds.

Juggernaut

The latest killstreak added is a slot 3 killstreak called the Juggernaut. The Juggernaut can replace the nuke as Slot 3 killstreak and can be called in on a 30 player killstreak. The killstreak is very different from the classic nuke however, as it is a whole new weapon just like the Slimer. This weapon quite literally makes the player a Juggernaut, boosting your HP to 2000 no matter what class you are using, and giving you a powerful minigun that can melt every player in your path with its impressive rate of fire. However, these upgrades do not come without any drawbacks, as your movement speed is reduced to about as slow as an LMG, and jumping is disabled, meaning that bhopping won't be possible. It's worth mentioning that there's no health regen while the Juggernaut is activated. You either run out of ammunition (250 - no reload) and return to your earlier class, or you die (run out of health).
